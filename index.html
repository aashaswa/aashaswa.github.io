Information about BMI Calculator

  • What is BMI?: Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.
  • How is BMI calculated?: BMI is calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. The formula is: BMI = weight (kg) / (height (m) * height (m))
  • Interpreting BMI values: BMI provides a simple numeric representation of a person's weight in relation to their height. However, it does not directly measure body fat and may not be accurate for everyone, particularly athletes or those with high muscle mass.
  • Factors to consider: It is important to interpret BMI values alongside other factors such as age, gender, and overall health to assess an individual's weight status accurately.
